WildSight is an app for iPhone and Android that allows users to record and export their wildlife sightings in common formats.
  • Records the location and time of the sightings.
  • Create your own list of species.
  • Take a picture with your phone.
  • Add a picture from the Photo Library after saving the sighting.
Exports sighting data to:
  • CSV for spreadsheets and statistics.
  • KML for mapping integration.
  • PDF for a simple report.
